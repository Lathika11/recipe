Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

This apple pie recipe has been in my familyfor generation and never fails to delight.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium-sized apples,peeled and siliced

Instructions

  1. preheat oven to 375 F(190 c)
  2. In a bowl,mix the sugar and cinamon.
  3. Toss the apples in the sugar-cinamon mixture.
